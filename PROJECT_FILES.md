## 📦 What Gets Submitted?

When you push to GitHub, everything goes:

✅ **Source code** (src/ folder)
✅ **Configuration** (package.json, etc.)
✅ **Documentation** (README.md, guides)
✅ **Screenshots** (your 4 images)
✅ **.gitignore** (keeps it clean)

❌ **NOT included** (thanks to .gitignore):
- `node_modules/` (too large, auto-installed)
- `dist/` (build output)
- `.env` files (if any)
- Editor configs
